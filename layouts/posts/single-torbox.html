Client

The Client: Nuvio

Nuvio is a Stremio-compatible streaming client with a cleaner UI and more customization than the stock Stremio app. Available on Android, iOS, and TV.

The key advantage: native debrid support. Connect TorBox once and it works across all your addons automatically — no need to paste your API key into every single addon config.

1
Open Nuvio and go to Settings
2
Navigate to Connected Services → TorBox
3
Paste your TorBox API key
4
Done. Every addon you add now benefits from this automatically.

Stream Addons

AIOStreams

The backbone of the setup. AIOStreams is a super-addon that pulls from multiple scrapers, deduplicates results, and sorts everything by quality. Saves you from seeing the same file listed 10 times from different sources.

Viren's hosted instance — reliable and fast

Add Torrentio and Comet as sources inside AIOStreams. Connect your TorBox API key there too so it can filter and prioritize cached results. AIOStreams Nightly has the latest features including full SEL (Stream Expression Language) for advanced filtering — worth using over stable.

Torrentio + Comet

The two best torrent scrapers. They cover different indexers so running both gives you much better hit rates on anything you search for.

⚠️

Important: when generating your Comet or Torrentio manifest URL, leave the debrid field empty. Since TorBox is already configured in Nuvio's Connected Services, it handles authentication automatically. Your manifest links stay clean and aren't tied to your credentials.

Fusion

Nuvio-native addon for home screen catalogs and widgets. Not required for streams but useful for organization if you want control over your home screen layout.

Tips

🔑

One API key, everywhere. Configure TorBox once in Nuvio Connected Services. Every addon benefits without you touching individual configs.

🧹

Clean manifest URLs. Never bake your debrid credentials into Torrentio or Comet manifest links. Nuvio handles it at the app level.

🛠

Use AIOStreams Nightly. It has the latest features including full SEL (Stream Expression Language) for advanced stream filtering and sorting.

⚡

Cache hit rates are real. TorBox's instant availability on popular content is genuinely impressive — most things play immediately without any wait.
