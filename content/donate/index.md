## Support Ember

Ember is a public archive — no paywalls, no ads, no tracking. Every guide, reference, and piece of documentation is freely available. If you have found something useful here and want to help keep the archive growing, your support is appreciated.

## Donate

[Support Ember on Ko-fi →](REPLACE_WITH_MY_KOFI_LINK)

Donations help cover the operational costs of running and maintaining this archive:

- Domain registration and renewal
- Hosting and infrastructure
- Testing environments for verifying configurations and workflows
- Development time for new guides, tool evaluations, and documentation
- Maintaining and updating existing content to keep it accurate

There is no donation goal and no campaign deadline — contribute whenever it makes sense for you.

## What You're Supporting

By supporting Ember, you are backing the idea that technical knowledge should be organized, accurate, and freely accessible. You are helping document solutions so others do not have to rediscover them from scratch. You are investing in clear documentation over fragmented forum threads, in verified configurations over outdated tutorials, and in a resource that prioritizes usefulness over engagement metrics.

## Other Ways to Help

Monetary support is not the only way to contribute. If the archive has been useful to you:

- **Share a guide** that solved a problem you had — someone else likely has the same question
- **Link to an article** from documentation, forums, or your own writing — inbound references help people discover the content
- **Report errors or outdated information** — documentation degrades, and corrections keep it sharp
- **Suggest improvements** — better explanations, missing steps, or clearer examples are always welcome
- **Recommend topics** — if something is missing from the archive, ask

## Transparency

A few things worth stating plainly:

- Donations are entirely optional — the archive remains free regardless
- All content stays publicly accessible — no supporter-only sections, no paywalled guides
- Support funds go toward maintaining and improving the archive — hosting, tooling, and the time required to produce accurate documentation

There is no subscription, no membership tier, and no recurring payment expectation. If you contribute once, thank you — that is enough.

## Thank You

To everyone who has used a guide, sent a correction, shared a link, or supported the archive in any form: this project exists because useful knowledge is worth documenting. Thank you for being part of that.
